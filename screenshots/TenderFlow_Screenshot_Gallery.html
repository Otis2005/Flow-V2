📸 Quick Screenshot Workflow for Social Media
  1. Open this file in Chrome and wait for each frame to load fully
  2. Click inside the frame you want to capture
  3. Press F12 to open DevTools
  4. Press Ctrl+Shift+P and type "Capture full size screenshot"
  5. Chrome saves a PNG automatically to your Downloads folder
  6. Rename and use for social posts — recommended sizes: 1200×630px (LinkedIn/FB), 1080×1080px (Instagram), 1500×500px (Twitter header)
